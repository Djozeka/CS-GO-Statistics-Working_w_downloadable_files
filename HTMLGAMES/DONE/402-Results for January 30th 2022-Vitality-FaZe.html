Contrary to what a lot of HLTV users think, bookmakers are very rarely involved with fixing matches. Instead, they make money by applying negative margins (as in, they pay out less money than they take in from BOTtors; you may have noticed that 50/50 odds are never 2/2). This means that they make more money, the more bets are made with them. The exception to this being if bets are made with them, where the BOTtor is skilled enough to beat the negative margins applied, OR a contest is rigged and someone places bets knowing the outcome, which is the only other way to beat the margins.

I recognize that there exist exceptions with some less reputable bookies (especially Chinese and Russian ones), but the vast, vast majority of fixes are conducted by players or teamsnot bookmakers. In fact, this belief is a great tell of someone who is completely clueless about the subject, like offliner228, above.
