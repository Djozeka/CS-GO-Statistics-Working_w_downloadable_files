Example:
Complexity have played against Vitality, IHC, Outsiders before so now we should look at the results of these teams to calculate the Buholz score, where one victory (for example, for Vitality) is +1 point for Complexity, and a loss is -1

Vitality - 3 wins: total score 3 points
IHC - 2 loses, 1 win; total score -1 point
Outsiders - 2 wins, 1 lose; Total score  1 point
Now we sum up these numbers to find out the Bucholz score for Complexity. 3+1-1=3

Now we do the same with IHC
 Astralis - 2 wins, 1 lose; score 1 point
Complexity - 2 loses and 1 win; score -1 point
Renegades - 3 losses; score -3ponts
